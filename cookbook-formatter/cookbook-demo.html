Strawberry French Toast

Ingredients

  • 200g Spaghetti
  • 100g Pancetta
  • 2 Eggs
  • 50g Parmesan
  • Salt & Pepper to taste

Instructions

  1. Boil the spaghetti in salted water.
  2. Fry the pancetta until crispy.
  3. Beat the eggs and mix with the cheese.
  4. Combine everything in a pan and serve.
